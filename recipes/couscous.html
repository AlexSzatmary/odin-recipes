Cous cous


Image: Lmmima

Description

Yummy cous cous

Ingredients

  • 1 package of cous cous
  • 2 cans of chickpeas
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 4 cucumbers
  • lemon juice
  • zaatar
  • sumac

Steps

  1. Prepare the cous cous according to package instructions.
  2. Saute the chickpeas in a little olive oil. Sprinkle with salt. Let the chickpeas get a little black. Squirt them with lemon juice.
  3. Chop the vegetables
  4. When the chickpeas are done, combine them with the vegetables. Season with zaatar.
  5. Serve.
