Macaroni


Image: ZiCheng Xu

Description

Yummy pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 box of pasta.
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce.

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water.
  2. Heat the tomato sauce.
  3. Add the pasta. Stir occasionally. Boil for 10 minutes or until done.
  4. Drain the pasta.
  5. Serve the pasta and tomato sauce together.
