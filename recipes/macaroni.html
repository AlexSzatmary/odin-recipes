Macaroni


Image: NowIsntItTime

Description

Yummy macaroni

Ingredients

  • 1 box of macaroni. Get the fancy organic stuff with the cheese as a sauce in a pouch.

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water.
  2. Add the pasta. Stir occasionally. Boil for 10 minutes or until done.
  3. Drain the pasta.
  4. Add the cheese sauce.
  5. Serve.
